## 1. Business Credit Profile & CIBIL Score

**What it is:**  
A business or personal **CIBIL score** reflects the creditworthiness of the borrower or business owner.

**Why it matters:**  
This is one of the key factors considered by lenders before approving a business loan.

**Preferred Score:**  
- **750+** → Best interest rates and fast approval  
- **700–749** → Good approval chances  
- **600–699** → Approval possible with higher interest rates  

**Important Note:**  
Even new businesses with limited credit history may qualify based on turnover, bank statements, and business stability.

---

## 2. Business Income & Turnover

Lenders evaluate the financial health of your business to ensure repayment capacity.

### For Self-Employed / Business Owners:
- Minimum **annual turnover** varies based on loan amount
- Regular income reflected in bank statements
- Profitable or stable business operations

### For Professionals & Proprietors:
- Consistent income from professional services
- Clean banking transactions

**Why this is important:**  
It assures lenders that the business can generate enough revenue to repay EMIs comfortably.

---

## 3. Business Stability & Vintage

**Minimum Business Age:**  
- Typically **2–3 years** of continuous operations is preferred

**Why lenders check this:**  
A stable business with consistent operations is considered lower risk compared to newly started ventures.

---

## 4. Existing Liabilities & Debt-to-Income Ratio

Lenders assess:
- Existing business loans
- Personal loans
- Credit card outstanding balances

**Rule of Thumb:**  
Total EMIs should **not exceed 50%** of the business or personal monthly income.

**Tip:**  
If your EMIs are already high, options like **loan restructuring or balance transfer** can improve approval chances.

---

## 5. Documentation Required (KYC & Financials)

### KYC Documents:
- **PAN Card** (Mandatory)
- **Aadhaar Card**

### Business Documents:
- Last **2 years’ ITR**
- Balance Sheet & Profit/Loss statement
- Bank statements (6–12 months)
- Business registration proof (GST, Shop Act, etc.)

Proper documentation ensures **faster approval and smoother processing**.

---

## 6. Relationship with Banks & Pre-Approved Offers

Businesses with an existing relationship with banks may receive:
- **Pre-approved business loan offers**
- Minimal or zero documentation
- Faster disbursal timelines

Maintaining a healthy banking relationship improves eligibility and terms.

---

## Summary Checklist for Business Loan Applicants

Before applying, ensure you can answer **yes** to most of these:

- Do I have a **good CIBIL score**?
- Is my business running for **at least 2 years**?
- Do I have **valid ITRs and bank statements**?
- Are my existing EMIs within manageable limits?
