# Home Loan – Complete Guide by Speedy Loan Finance Services

A **Home Loan** helps you fulfill your dream of owning a house by providing financial support to purchase, construct, or renovate a residential property. With long repayment tenures and affordable EMIs, home loans are one of the most cost-effective borrowing options available today.

At **Speedy Loan Finance Services**, we help you secure the best home loan offers with competitive interest rates and a smooth, transparent process.

---

## 1. Credit Score (CIBIL Score)

**What it is:**  
Your **CIBIL score** represents your creditworthiness and repayment history.

**Why it matters:**  
This is a crucial factor in determining eligibility and interest rates for a home loan.

**Preferred Score Range:**  
- **750+** → Best interest rates and fast approval  
- **700–749** → Good approval chances  
- **600–699** → Approval possible with higher interest rates  

A higher score significantly reduces your overall interest burden.

---

## 2. Income & Repayment Capacity

Lenders evaluate your income to ensure you can comfortably repay the home loan EMI over the long term.

### For Salaried Individuals:
- Stable monthly income
- Employer profile and job continuity

### For Self-Employed Individuals:
- Consistent business income
- Last **2–3 years’ ITR**
- Profitability and cash flow stability

**Why this is important:**  
Home loans are long-term commitments, so lenders ensure sustainable repayment capacity.

---

## 3. Employment & Business Stability

**Salaried Applicants:**  
- Minimum **1–2 years of total work experience**
- At least **6–12 months** with the current employer

**Self-Employed Applicants:**  
- Business vintage of **2–3 years**

Stability assures lenders of consistent income.

---

## 4. Existing Loans & EMI Obligations

Lenders consider:
- Existing home loans
- Car loans
- Personal loans
- Credit card dues

**EMI Rule:**  
Total EMIs (including proposed home loan EMI) should generally **not exceed 50–60%** of monthly income.

Lower existing liabilities increase loan eligibility.

---

## 5. Property Evaluation & Legal Check

For home loans, the property itself is evaluated:

- Legal title verification  
- Builder/project approval  
- Property valuation by bank-approved valuer  

This ensures the property is legally clear and financially safe.

---

## 6. Documentation Required

### KYC Documents:
- **Aadhaar Card**
- **PAN Card**

### Income Documents:
- Salary slips (last 3 months)  
- Bank statements (last 6 months)  
- ITR (for self-employed applicants)

### Property Documents:
- Sale agreement  
- Allotment letter  
- Approved building plan  

Complete documentation speeds up approval and disbursal.

---

## 7. Bank Relationship & Pre-Approved Offers

Applicants with a strong relationship with a bank may receive:
- **Pre-approved home loan offers**
- Faster processing
- Minimal documentation

Maintaining a healthy banking history improves loan terms.

---

## Summary Checklist for Home Loan Applicants

Before applying, ensure:
- Your **CIBIL score is 700+**
- You have stable income
- Property documents are legally clear
- Existing EMIs are manageable
