# Loan Against Property – Smart Financing Using Your Property

A **Loan Against Property (LAP)** allows you to raise substantial funds by mortgaging your residential or commercial property while retaining ownership. It offers **lower interest rates, higher loan amounts, and longer repayment tenure** compared to unsecured loans.

At **Speedy Loan Finance Services**, we help you secure the best LAP offers with a smooth, transparent process.

---

## 1. Credit Score (CIBIL Score)

**What it is:**  
Your **CIBIL score** reflects your creditworthiness and repayment behavior.

**Why it matters:**  
A good score improves eligibility and reduces interest rates.

**Preferred Score:**  
- **750+** → Best rates & faster approval  
- **700–749** → Good approval chances  
- **600–699** → Approval possible with higher rates  

Even with an average score, LAP may still be approved due to property backing.

---

## 2. Property Value & Type

Lenders assess:
- Market value of the property  
- Residential or commercial usage  
- Location and legal status  

**Loan Amount:**  
Typically **50%–70%** of the property’s market value.

---

## 3. Income & Repayment Capacity

### Salaried Individuals:
- Stable monthly income
- Long-term employment continuity

### Self-Employed / Business Owners:
- Regular business income
- Last **2–3 years’ ITR**
- Strong cash flow

Lenders ensure EMI affordability over long tenure.

---

## 4. Employment & Business Stability

**Salaried Applicants:**  
- Minimum **1–2 years of work experience**
- At least **6–12 months** in current job

**Self-Employed Applicants:**  
- Business vintage of **2–3 years**

Stability reduces lender risk and improves loan terms.

---

## 5. Existing Loans & EMI Obligations

Total EMIs (including LAP EMI) should generally **not exceed 50–60%** of monthly income.

Lower liabilities increase approval chances and loan amount.

---

## 6. Property Legal & Technical Verification

Banks conduct:
- Legal title check  
- Encumbrance verification  
- Property valuation by approved valuer  

Clear property documents are mandatory for disbursal.

---

## 7. Documentation Required

### KYC Documents:
- **Aadhaar Card**
- **PAN Card**

### Income Documents:
- Salary slips / ITR
- Bank statements (6–12 months)

### Property Documents:
- Sale deed  
- Property tax receipts  
- Approved building plan  

Complete documentation speeds up approval.

---

## Summary Checklist for LAP Applicants

Before applying, ensure:
- Property is legally clear
- CIBIL score is **700+**
- Stable income source
- Existing EMIs are manageable
