# Working Capital Loan – Powering Day-to-Day Business Operations

A **Working Capital Loan** is designed to help businesses manage their **daily operational expenses**, including inventory purchase, vendor payments, salaries, rent, utilities, and short-term cash flow gaps.

At **Speedy Loan Finance Services**, we help businesses access fast and flexible working capital solutions through **India’s leading banks and NBFC partners**, ensuring uninterrupted business growth.

---

## 1. What Is a Working Capital Loan?

A working capital loan provides funds to meet **short-term business requirements**, ensuring that operations continue smoothly even during seasonal slowdowns or delayed receivables.

It is ideal for:
- Paying suppliers and vendors  
- Managing inventory cycles  
- Covering salary and overhead costs  
- Handling short-term cash crunches  

---

## 2. Types of Working Capital Facilities

### Fund-Based Facilities:
- Cash Credit (CC)
- Overdraft (OD)
- Short-Term Business Loans

### Non-Fund-Based Facilities:
- Bank Guarantee (BG)
- Letter of Credit (LC)

Our experts help you choose the **right facility based on your business model**.

---

## 3. Eligibility Criteria

### Credit Profile (CIBIL Score):
- **750+** → Best interest rates & higher limits  
- **700–749** → Good approval chances  
- **600–699** → Approval possible with conditions  

### Business Vintage:
- Minimum **2–3 years** of continuous operations

### Turnover:
- Stable and regular business turnover
- Healthy banking transactions

---

## 4. Income & Cash Flow Assessment

Lenders analyze:
- Monthly inflows and outflows  
- Average bank balance  
- Receivable and payable cycles  

This ensures the business can service interest and repayments comfortably.

---

## 5. Documentation Required

### KYC Documents:
- **PAN Card**
- **Aadhaar Card**

### Business Documents:
- Last **2–3 years’ ITR**
- Profit & Loss statement & Balance Sheet
- Bank statements (last 6–12 months)
- GST returns (if applicable)
- Business registration proof (GST / Shop Act / MSME)

Complete documentation enables **faster approval and limit enhancement**.
