# Lease Rental Discounting (LRD) – Monetize Your Rental Income

**Lease Rental Discounting (LRD)** is a secured loan facility offered against **commercial properties that generate stable rental income**. Under this facility, lenders provide loans based on the **future rental cash flows** from leased properties.

At **Speedy Loan Finance Services**, we help property owners, developers, and businesses unlock the value of their leased assets with structured and cost-effective LRD solutions.

---

## 1. What Is Lease Rental Discounting (LRD)?

LRD is a funding solution where:
- The loan is sanctioned against **leased commercial property**
- Repayment is directly linked to **monthly rental income**
- The lease agreement with a reputed tenant forms the base of eligibility

It is ideal for property owners seeking large-ticket funding without selling their asset.

---

## 2. Eligible Properties & Tenants

### Eligible Properties:
- Commercial offices  
- IT parks  
- Retail outlets  
- Warehouses & industrial units  

### Preferred Tenants:
- MNCs  
- Corporates  
- Reputed companies with long-term leases  

Stronger tenants improve loan eligibility and interest rates.

---

## 3. Key Eligibility Criteria

### Credit Profile (CIBIL Score):
- **750+** → Best interest rates and higher loan amounts  
- **700–749** → Good approval chances  
- **Below 700** → Case-to-case basis  

### Lease Agreement:
- Registered lease agreement
- Remaining lease tenure of **3–5 years or more**
- Stable and predictable rental income

---

## 4. Loan Amount & Tenure

**Loan Amount:**
- Typically **60%–75%** of the property’s rental value or discounted cash flow

**Tenure:**
- Usually aligned with the lease tenure
- Ranges from **5 to 15 years**

Higher rental stability results in better loan terms.

---

## 5. Rental Income & Cash Flow Assessment

Lenders analyze:
- Monthly rental receipts  
- Lease escalation clauses  
- Vacancy risk  
- Credit profile of tenant  

This ensures reliable loan servicing through rental inflows.

---

## 6. Documentation Required

### KYC Documents:
- **PAN Card**
- **Aadhaar Card**

### Property & Lease Documents:
- Registered lease agreement  
- Rent receipts & bank statements  
- Property ownership documents  
- Approved building plans  

### Financial Documents:
- ITR for last **2–3 years**
- Bank statements (6–12 months)

Complete documentation enables faster processing and sanction.
